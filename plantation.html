TREE PLANTATION

"The care of the Earth is our most ancient and most worthy, and after all, our most pleasing responsibility. ”

― Wendell Berry

Rotaract Club hosted Tree plantation drive on 09.10.2017 in Thapar University near Heritage Haveli. It was graced by the presence of Dr Sawraj Singh, cardiovascular surgeon who even after practicing outside India for about 50 years is close to his roots. He addressed the students and told them about how trees teach Tolerance , persistence , patience and contentment.
