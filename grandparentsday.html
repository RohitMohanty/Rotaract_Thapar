"Nobody can do for little children what grandparents do. Grandparents sort of sprinkle stardust over the lives of little children.”

Rotaract Club of TIET paid a visit to the Old Age Home in Patiala which we visited earlier to celebrate Grandparents Day with the members of our extended family there. And we wish Happy Grandparents Day to all the elders and we hope to seek your blessings in every possible way ❤️
