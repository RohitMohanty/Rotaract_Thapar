PUKAAR

Rotaract club of TIET organized Rotary District 3090's annual conference Pukaar'18 which witnessed coming together of all the clubs of the district to fulfill the agenda Together Stronger. PDG Rtn Vijay Gupta who was the chief guest of the event was accompanied by Guest of honour DG ND Vijay Arora, PDG Dharamvir Garg, DRCC Rajiv Mittal and DG Elect VB Dixit.

This event witnessed people from Rotary and different districts coming together to discuss and work on the challenges faced while running a club.

The event ended by a vote of thanks given by the host club president. The conference was successful and gave an overview of all the clubs in the district and their work and also cleared a lot of doubts Rotaractors have had.
