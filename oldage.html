OLD AGE HOME

"Let us never know what old age is. Let us know the happiness time brings, not count the years.”

― Ausonius

Rotaract Club of TIET organised a health check camp for elderly people at Bhai Kanhaiya Charitable Trust old age home, Near Sullar Road, Patiala in association with Vishva Hindu Parishad where in free medicines were distributed as per doctor's recommendation. Dr Vikas Goyal from Mata Kaushalya Devi Hospital along with orthopaedic and Ayurvedic specialist Doctors paid a visit and checked all the in house patients. A team of 30 club members interacted with the inmates and shared insights of their lives. A lot of emotional and happy moments were shared by the club members with the seniors. We would be visiting them frequently because now they are a member of our family here on.
