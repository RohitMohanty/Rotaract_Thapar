INSTALLATION CEREMONY

"Self Development - Fellowship Through Service”

― Rotaract

Rotaract Club of TIET had it's first ever President and Council Installation on 10.09.18. The evening comprised of collaring the president Vanita Bakshi and pinning of council members by our teacher mentors. Event was followed by the words of guidance by Navdeep ma'am and Sonam ma'am especially to new recruits. Council members also interacted with the students to give them an idea of working of the club. Event was summed up in a group picture which consisted of 100 members of the fam.
