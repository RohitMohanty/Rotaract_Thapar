SANITATION DRIVE 2

"My suggestion is that we should first work to ensure the Third World has clean drinking water and sanitation.”

― Bjorn Lomborg

After the success of Sanitation Drive conducted earlier, Rotaract Club of Thapar University organised the second edition of Sanitation Drive in Truck Union Slums wherein enthusiastic new recruits and the old ones actively took part in. Almost 75 packets of sanitary packets were distributed and special emphasis was laid on good Disposal methods for which newspapers were distributed and females were made aware of the same.
