BLOOD DONATION CAMP

"The Blood You Donate Gives Someone Another Chance At Life. One Day That Someone May Be A Close Relative, A Friend, A Loved One—Or Even You.”

― Redcrossblood.org

Rotaract Club of T.I.E.T in association with Rajindra Hospital organised a Blood Donation Camp in Thapar Institute of Engineering and technology on 30.01.2018 and recieved a great response from students and faculty for the same. A total of 135 blood units were collected and were sent for the noble cause. We would like to Thank all those who came forward and donated the blood and we would also urge people to keep doing the noble cause because we never know whose life we could save.
