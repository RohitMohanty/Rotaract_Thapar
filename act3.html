HEALTH CHECKUP CAMP

"To keep the body in good health is a duty... otherwise we shall not be able to keep our mind strong and clear.”

― Buddha

Free Health Check up Camp was being organised by Rotaract Club TU in Thapar University to keep a check on Dental , Eyes and Blood sugar ailments. Hundreds of people turned up to get themselves checked and majority of them were in healthy and sound condition. People also signed a pledge to keep themselves healthy and lead a happy life . We hope you all get yourselves checked regularly and are in Pink of your health as well :)
