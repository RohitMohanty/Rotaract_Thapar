Rotaract Club of Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology

Rotaract Club is a youth run social services platform. Worldwide there are more than 10,000 clubs with over 2 lakh Rotaractors dedicated to help this community become a better place to live for others and for themselves.

We at Rotaract Club of TIET believe the same. Our club's motto is "Remodeling a better tomorrow" through which passionate people come together to exchange ideas to tackle communities' most persistent problems, provide aid to people in need, spread prosperity and invest in the next generation for tomorrow by organising fun activities like Health check-up camps, distributing sanitary napkins in slums,spreading cancer awareness, visit to old age home, awareness camps in schools amongst many.
